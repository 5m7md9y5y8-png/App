2. You see 3 sound type buttons

### Generate a Tone:
1. Pick a sound type (Pure/Harmonic/Chirp)
2. Pick a frequency (440Hz-4000Hz)
3. Tap **▶️ Preview** to hear it
4. Tap **⏹️ Stop** to stop

### Apply as Notification Sound:
1. Once you like a tone
2. Tap **✅ Apply to Notifications**
3. It becomes your notification sound!

## 🎵 Sound Types Explained

### Pure Tone 🔔
- Single clean frequency
- Smooth & soft
- Like a bell tone
- Best for: Professional settings

### Harmonic Tone 🎼
- Multiple frequencies mixed
- Rich & musical
- Fuller sound
- Best for: Pleasant notifications

### Chirp Tone 📈
- Ascending frequency sweep
- Starts low, goes high
- Very distinctive
- Best for: Attention-grabbing

**A:** Yes! Create different sounds by selecting different frequencies and applying them.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### App won't install
- Make sure you have enough storage (100MB+)
- Try deleting some apps and retry
- Restart your phone and retry

### No sound when previewing
- Check volume is ON (not muted)
- Check "Do Not Disturb" is OFF
- Close other apps playing music
- Restart the app

### App crashes
- Uninstall the app
- Restart your phone
- Reinstall the app

### Notification sound didn't change
- Make sure you tapped "Apply to Notifications"
- Restart your phone
- Check notification settings in your phone's Settings app
